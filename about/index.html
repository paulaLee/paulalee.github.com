About Us

CINEBITES

CINEBITES is an annual Film Festival held in Denver, CO. We showcase short films from around the world.

Since 2009 the festival has been a platform for independent filmmakers to network and share their work with the community. The festival functions as a fundraiser for the non-profit Arts Street (arts-street.org) Films are curated by members of the Arts Street Alumni Association who strive to exhibit great short films from many genres. Email one of our friendly organizers if you have any questions or would like to get involved.

Arts Street

Arts Street started in 1999 as a non-profit youth program in Denver that creates opportunities for the young people of Colordao. The opportunities provided are designed to foster the inspiration and education of these youth to become tomorrow's professionals and supporting the workforce needs. This is achieced by developing and implementing highly diversified career-based, educational programs that incorporate real world learning, artistic expression and creative thinking.

Paula Lee

Organizer and Web Woman

Paula Lee started with Arts Street as a participant in a documentary workshop in 2005. She created "The Average Asian" at the age of 17, learning stop motion animation, green screen tenhnology and a multitude of mediums in order to accomplish her short film. After having her film showcased along with 9 other participants at the Buntport theater, Fusionbox, one of the top web design and development companies in the nation asked if Lee could intern with them part-time. She has now been with the company since 2006, and holds the position as Head Quality Assurance Specialist. She's continued to stay with Arts Street to support the arts & currently holds the position as Arts Street's Secretary of the Board of Directors.

Lee believes in building communities and giving back to society. Some know her as being ascetic. She has worked with and has held leadership positions with numerous local non-profit organizations. Some include PeaceJam and AFSC. Her hobbies include: volunteering, knitting, coding, & brainstorming new ways of bettering society.
